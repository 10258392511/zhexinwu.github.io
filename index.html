ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Zhexin (Tony) Wu. I have completed the CFA Program Level I. I'm currenly a third-year MSc student at the Department of Management, Technology and Economics (MTEC) at ETH Zürich. Passionate about merging technology and finance, I focus on developing explainable, data-driven quant trading strategies rooted in deep insights from optimization, machine learning, and strong fundamentals.

Prior to this, I achieved an MSc with distinction from the Department of Information Technology and Electrical Engineering (EEIT) at ETH Zürich. Preceding that, I spent a year as an MS student within the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor. Both at ETH Zürich and the University of Michigan, my focus has been on Signal Processing & Machine Learning, specializing in optimization methods and deep learning for biomedical image processing and analysis.
